(vi) Trigonometric identity proof:​

(i) Sphere melted into wire:​

- Equating volumes: 0.01πh = 36π → h = 36/0.01 = 3600 cm = 36 m [4] 
(ii) Proportion equation:​

- 4(x+5) = 25(x-16) → 4x + 20 = 25x - 400​
- 4x - 25x = -400 - 20 → -21x = -420 → x = 20​
- Check: √(25) + √(4) = 5 + 2 = 7, √(25) - √(4) = 5 - 2 = 3, ratio 7/3 ✓ [3]
