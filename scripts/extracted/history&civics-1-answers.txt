(iii) Distinction between Council of Ministers and Cabinet:‚Äã
- Council of Ministers: It is a large body consisting of all ministers (Cabinet Ministers, 
Ministers of State, and Deputy Ministers). It does not meet as a body. It is collectively

- Cabinet: It is a smaller, inner body of senior ministers within the Council of Ministers. It

important matters. [2]

null and void. [1.5]‚Äã
Importance: It acts as a guardian of the Constitution and protects the fundamental 
rights of citizens from legislative and executive overreach. [1.5] 
(iii) Advantages of Lok Adalats:‚Äã
- They provide speedy justice as cases are disposed of quickly.‚Äã
- There are no court fees involved, making justice affordable.‚Äã
- They work on the principle of compromise and conciliation, which helps in amicable 
settlements.‚Äã
- The decision of a Lok Adalat is binding and has the force of a decree of a civil court, 
with no appeal against it.‚Äã
